About Career Navigator

Career Navigator is a student-focused career guidance platform designed to help engineering students explore career opportunities, identify skill gaps, build professional resumes, discover learning resources, and prepare for future job opportunities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a single platform where students can access career guidance, learning resources, certifications, job opportunities, and resume-building tools to achieve their professional goals.

Key Features

  • Resume Builder
  • Certificates Hub
  • Career Roadmaps
  • Latest Job Opportunities
  • Skill Gap Analyzer
  • Career Recommendation System
  • Learning Resources
